Grouting Design for Ground Improvement in Kilkenny

Together, we solve the challenges of tomorrow.

LEARN MORE →

The Castlecomer Road extension project hit a pocket of weathered limestone with open fissures at just four metres depth. Water inflow stopped the excavation for three days. We see this across Kilkenny — the Carboniferous limestone here fractures unpredictably, especially near the River Nore floodplain. A proper grouting design maps those discontinuities before injection begins. Our lab team designs the mix, sets the pressure limits, and defines the grid spacing. We do not guess. The plate load test quantifies improvement after treatment, confirming modulus values onsite.

Grouting in Kilkenny limestone demands pressure control within 2 bar tolerance — exceed it and you fracture the formation.

Scope of work

In Kilkenny city centre jobs, the drift geology layers glacial till over karst limestone. You cannot use a single grout type across the whole site. We specify permeation grouting with microfine cements in the silty till, then switch to compaction grouting at the rockhead. For the deep solution features under St Canice's parish zone we have used sleeve-pipe grouting at 15-bar pressure. Every design stage follows IS EN 12715:2020 execution standard. Our lab runs rheology checks on each batch. Viscosity stays under 30 seconds Marsh cone. Bleed below 2%. These are hard numbers, not estimates.

Local geotechnical context

Kilkenny sits at 60 metres elevation with a rainfall average of 870 mm per year. That water moves fast through the karst system. Uncontrolled grout travel is the main site risk. We have tracked grout emerging 30 metres from the injection point during a river wall project on the Nore. The design must include real-time pressure-volume logging and pre-set refusal criteria. Our approach includes stage grouting and packer isolation at every 2-metre interval. No shortcuts. A missed fissure under a foundation in Kilkenny means differential settlement that appears fast.

Top questions

What does grouting design cost for a typical Kilkenny site?

Design packages for grouting in the Kilkenny area run from €1,110 to €3,500. The range depends on number of borehole logs reviewed, grout type trials required, and whether the site has karst features needing extra investigation.

How do you determine the correct grout type for Kilkenny limestone?

We start with the Lugeon or packer permeability test data. Values under 10 Lugeon can take microfine permeation grout. Between 10 and 30 Lugeon we move to compaction grouting. Above that, or where cavities are proven, we design jet grouting columns. Rock quality designation from core logs guides the final call.

What pressure limits apply to grouting near existing buildings in Kilkenny?

We cap injection pressure at 1.0 to 1.5 times the overburden stress at that depth. In the city centre, near medieval structures on High Street, we add heave monitoring points and set a 2 mm movement alert. Pressure is reduced if the flow rate exceeds 5 litres per minute during permeation work.
